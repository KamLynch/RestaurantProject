Discover Our Story

Our locally owned neighborhood restaurant offers a seasonal menu of inventive Hispanic fresh cuisine cooked simply and deliciously in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Specializing in farm to table favorites with a unique twist, we serve lunch and dinner along with weekend brunch. The space pays tribute to the taste and sounds of Mexico and updates it into a cozy series of rooms that temper elegance with rustic finishes. In addition to our tavern and dining room, we offer also offer a heated, covered patio and a private dining and event space. With a full bar, live music, trivia, bingo and a game room for children of all ages, we are the perfect destination for any occasion.
